1.) Call Of Duty

Call of Duty is a FPS video game owned by Activision. Created in 2003, it focused on games set in World War II. Later, the series has seen games set in the midst of the Cold War. The games were first owned by Infinity Ward followed by Treyarch and Sledgehammer Games. Several spin-off games were made by other developers. The current one Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, was released October 25, 2019. The next game, Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War, is said to be released on November 13, 2020.

2.) Madden

Madden NFL is an football game based on the NFL created by EA Tiburon and published by Electronic Arts. It was released for most major video game systems on August 28, 2020, and will be available for all at a later date. It includes Baltimore Ravens qb Lamar Jackson as the cover athlete. The game received mixed reviews from critics, who loved its gameplay but disliked the amount of technical issues.

3.) Rainbow Six Seige

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is an tactical shooter video game created by Ubisoft Montreal and published by the same. It was available on some major platforms on December 1, 2015; it is going to be available on most major platforms in 2020. The game relies on destruction and user cooperation. Each player is in control of an attacker or a defender in different gameplay missions such as saving hostages, defusing a bomb, and taking over an object in the room.
